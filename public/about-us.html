What is VideooCV ?

Videoocv.com is a Job portal for both the recruiters and job seekers looking for an easy way of recruitment process. The site has been designed in such a way that all of the job seekers can upload video cv’s , visual cv ‘s who are looking for an opportunity can find their willing job in a very effective and efficient manner by uploading self-recorded Video CV or Video CV recorded through us in a minimal amount of time , and beauty of this is employer or recruiter can skip first round of interview. And similarly, all the recruiters can find the right candidates at the right time for the job posted filtering the unnecessary steps in their traditional recruitment process .

Videoocv.com is all about Video CV, Visual CV and Video JD

India is a densely populated country. With a population of over 1.2 billion it is not at all hard to guess how many people apply for a job every day. But have you ever imagined how long it takes to read those thousands of CVs for a recruiter and then selecting the best ones from the same? Similarly there are thousands of jobs posted every day and it is very cumbersome to find out the most suitable one for a candidate. That is why Videoocv.com came up with the idea of Video CV for the recruiters and Video JD for the candidates .
