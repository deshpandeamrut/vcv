The VCV Recruiter Process

Mortgage Chance provides investors with a marketplace to enter into vendor finance, instalment contract agreements with borro wers. This type of investment is focused on cash flow positive returns instead of other property investments which focus on Capital Growth speculation.

The Mortgage Chance model involves investors purchasing a property (using a traditional mortgage if required) and then sellin g it immediately (under a vendor finance contract) directly to the borrower who does not qualify for a loan.

In contrast to a traditional contract of sale, instead of receiving the full asking price from the borrower (with a traditional settlement period 30-60 days) the contract instead specifies that the borrower repay the investor by way of monthly repayments (instalments - agreed with the borrower) over a period of 25 - 30 years. These monthly repayments are higher than the monthly repayments paid by the investor to the lending institution, which by definition, makes instalment contract property investment positive cash flow for the investor. It is the expectation that the borrower will refinance with a traditional le nder as soon as they have built enough equity in the property (usually within 5 years). Instalment contract investing is therefore seen as a medium to high yielding in vestment with a medium investment horizon. Instalment contract investing can be seen as an alternative to purchasing a property as a buy-to-let (rent).

Mortgage Chance facilitates a process whereby borrowers provide investors with their personal profile and details of the prop erty they wish to purchase. Investors review the borrower profiles and associated property and if interested, put their best financing proposal forward to the bor rower for consideration. Once an agreement has been made "in principal" Mortgage Chance works with both investor and borrower to finalise the contract and then provide ongoing management services for the benefit of both parties.

With Mortgage Chance, investors will be provided with top tier legal documentation to support the instalment contract, have security over the property and benefit from a cash flow positive investment. Instalment contracts offer investors a lucrative cash flow positive alternative to tra ditional direct property investment.

The Investor Journey

Adam and Stacy are first time property investors with equity in their home and are keen to buy an investment property. They have heard of great returns available through Mortgage Chance.

Adam and Stacy speak to their banker and confirm the amount and the percentage interest rate they can borrow.

Adam and Stacy sign up to Mortgage Chance and view properties and second chance borrowers in their local area.

Adam and Stacy have found a property and borrower profile for which they think is a good investment within their budget.

The borrower approves Adam and Stacy’s proposal on the property.

Adam and Stacy offer the borrowers a 1.5% higher rate than the rate from their bank and mark the property sale price up 3%. They also specified a fixed term of 5 years.

Adam and Stacy are approved for finance to purchase the property. At the same time they enter into a contract with the borrower to sell them the property through an instalment contract.

With the assistance of Mortgage Chance, both parties sign the contract. The borrower agrees to purchase the property from Adam and Stacy through monthly instalment payments. Adam and Stacy receive $600 per month net income from the investment.
Mortgage Chance provides management services to each party for the duration of the contract.

After 5 years the property has increased in value and the borrower is now able to refinance with a bank and finalise the contract agreement with Adam and Stacy.
