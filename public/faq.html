Frequently asked questions

How does VideoCV work for an Employee ?

VideoCV is an online career site. The service platform provides innovative online recruitment tools for companies and jobseekers, making video job posting and finding a job more presentable ,interactive, easy and efficient. Registered companies can post job postings which jobseekers can then find and apply for. Under no circumstances are we involved in any way in the selection process of the company. Every company uses its own processes and procedures. In order to take full advantage of our services, employers and jobseekers must first register on our website.

What can I do in after registration ?

From your Account you can see at a glance your latest applications, your default video Resume, etc. Your Account allows you to see an overview of your activities on the site.

How do I apply for a job posting?

f you have found an interesting posting and believe that your resume fits with the company's requirements, then you can apply for it by sending them your video resume & Document (.doc, .pdf etc) resume.

How can I check the status of my job applications?

You can check which stage in the selection process your application has reached by going to "My Account". Under "My Applications", you will then see one of the following labels next to each entry :

  • Received: the company has received your Resume but has not yet reviewed it.
  • In process: you have gone through to the selection stage but it does not mean you have been selected.
  • Discarded: the company thinks your Resume is not a good match for the position.
  • Finalist: the company is interested in your Resume and you have to wait for them to contact you to inform you whether or not you have been offered the job

    I registered with Videoocv.com but haven't been contacted by any companies. Why?

    Companies carry out their recruitment processes according to their own internal policies. Videoocv.com is only responsible for posting the job vacancies and maintaining the database containing candidate resumes. We have nothing to do with the company's internal recruitment processes. You can find the status of your applications in your dashboard (My Account).

    Some companies contact all the applicants by telephone or email, whether they intend to interview them or not. Others only contact those they have selected as being "Finalists". However, even then, not all companies actually make contact with all the finalists.

    If you have registered with videooc.com but you haven't applied for any positions posted by companies you will most likely not receive many job offers. You have to apply for a position that has been posted in order for the company to know that you are interested in the vacancy.

    On the Videoocv.com home page, you will see a search box where you can specify the fields for your search (by entering a keyword and the state where you want to work). Once you have done a search, select the posting(s) you are interested in. To send your video Resume to a company, click on "Apply for this position". The system will ask you to enter your email address and password. You can then select the Resume you wish to send along with a Cover Letter (optional). The company will receive your application and then contact you should it wish to continue with the process.

    How can we register as a company?

    From the videoocv.com click on "Employer post a job". Carefully complete the registration form . The fields marked with a red asterisks are compulsory

    Once you have completed the process you are then registered and can begin posting vacancies. However, these won't appear on the page until you have received a telephone call from the videoocv.com Customer Service department to validate your registration details. This process is essential in order to assure our candidates that the companies offering employment are reliable

    This process only needs to be done once. Once your company details have been finished the registration process you will be able to access My Account using your email and password. Here you can change your login details, edit, modify or delete job postings, and view the resumes of the candidates who have responded to your vacancies.

    How do we change the details of a posting?

    Click on the "Sign In" link and enter your email and password to access My Account.

    SIn My Account you will see a list of all the vacancies you have posted. At the top of the page, you will find a number of boxes that enable you to filter the postings (active, inactive, draft or all).

    Each offer is shown on a separate row in the list. Clicking on "Edit" will open the offer form and allow you to change any fields you choose. Remember, however, that you are unable to modify the "Position", "Country" or "Category" fields.

    When you have made the changes you require, simply click on "Save". Your offer will now be updated with any changes you made.

    I registered with Videoocv.com but haven't been contacted by any companies. Why?

    When a candidate applies for your job vacancy, you will receive the application in your private account.

    We recommend that you manage the applications you have received by moving them to the appropriate folder (processed, rejected, or finalist) so that you can see at a glance what candidates are in which stage of the selection process. By moving the applications to the corresponding folders, you also provide information to the candidate who is monitoring the status is the application. If you move someone to the Finalist folder s/he will expect you to contact him/her by e-mail or telephone.

    You can contact candidates directly by using the data contained in the application.

    Can we use a quick filter to view the resumes ?

    When reviewing the resumes received in response to an offer, you will see a list of all the candidates and three columns showing their professional experience, education and place of residence. If you select any of these elements, the system will filter the candidates to only show the details you have specified.The filter works in real time and enables you to quickly view specific details for the candidates you are most interested in .

    How long can a posting remain online?

    An offer can be posted for 60 days, although this period can also be prolonged if required. When this time expires the offer is autoarchived as "Inactive" and will not be visible to candidates.

    If you wish to stop your postings from being removed from the website, you can do this in the 5-day period prior to their expiry date. If you edit the posting during this period, you will have the option to "Renew". Selecting this option will mean your postings will be visible for another 60 days.

    In the "My job postings" section select the icon "Edit" for the posting you wish to renew.

    DO you check background checks of candidate who apply through videoocv.com ?

    No Videoocv.com is only a dedicated recruitment facilitating portal , where candidates background checks are not done.
